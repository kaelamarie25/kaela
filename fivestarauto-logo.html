Five Star Auto Sales

freelance / logo

Logo design commissioned for a startup auto sales business. I had complete creative freedom, with the exception that I use this purple-blue color. I wanted to maintain a minimalist and updated feel in the logo design. So I decided to create the shape of a car out of negative space, and keep all text items blcoked out and aligned.
