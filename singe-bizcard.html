Singe

freelance / branding / print

A popular Boston-area saxophonist is starting his own band, and he came to me to develop his logo and business cards. The red symbol in the logo is a quarter rest, but also mimics the shape of a flame - perfect for a band called Singe. The business cards were designed after the client's personality and business goals: classic (in layout and color), but with a twist of movement and drama (which come through in the photograph).
