Pictorial Pamphlet

marketing

This is another of my favorite projects: creating a pictorial pamphlet for use as a free informational download. The goal in creating this project was to use large, impactful imagery, which I relied on more heavily than copy, so that it might drive someone to contact us to learn more. A secondary goal of this was to provide an overview of who NutriSavings is and what the program offers so that the sales team could refer back to the same document and present a solid, unified message to potential clients.
