Grilling Safety

infographic

One of the first projects I was assigned at NutriSavings was to design an infographic about grilling safety to be included in one of our monthly newsletters (which you can check out here). Looking back, there are some things I would tweak about the design now, but I decided to leave it as it was originally designed to show where I started. Collecting imagery for this as I thought about layout was enjoyable. And, similar to when I designed the lactose intolerance infographic, I really enjoyed designing the little fire and warning symbol icons. It's about the small things! ;)
