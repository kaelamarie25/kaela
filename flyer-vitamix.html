Handout / Vitamix Promotion

flyer / marketing

I was tasked with creating two B2B flyers to be handed out at an event. The flyers had to inform the audience about the company, and drive them to visit a landing page to download more information. We included a Vitamix promotional offer to incentivize them to download the materials. I had to work within Vitamix's brand guidelines, and come up with a design that was engaging enough to make someone want to not only pick it up, but to actually carry out our desired action (visiting the landing page) as well.
