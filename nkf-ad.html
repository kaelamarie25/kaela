One of NutriSavings' partners is the National Kidney Foundation, who hosts an annual celebratory event. I was tasked with designing a 1/4 page full-color ad for NutriSavings to appear in the program book for the 2017 event. At the time, there was no copywriter for the marketing department, so I'm proud to say I created the copy as well as the design for this piece.
