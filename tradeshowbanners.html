Trade Show Banners

marketing

As part of the rebrand process at NutriSavings, I was tasked with redesigning these roll-up trade show banners. In the past, these banners were too text-heavy, and therefore ineffective. At trade shows, people are walking around and passing a lot of booths. To grab their attention, it might be better to display large imagery and stats instead. Also, the past banners' design required that all 3 be set up at all times. Sometimes, there are space limitations, so I wanted to design them so that only 2 could be used at a time if needed; I wanted them to be able to stand on their own.
