Childrens' Activity Booklets

print-intent

Upon first starting at NutriSavings, my whimsical and childlike design style was pretty immediately recognized, and so I was approached to develop childrens' activity booklets. The three booklets were meant to be used as downloadable content and giveaway material for lead generation. The goal of each is to get kids excited and educated about food and nurition through various activities, such as word finds and mazes. Each of the three booklets has its own theme. The first is adventure-themed: Kid Huckabee needs help identifying foods and their health benefits. The second is garden-themed: Paisley needs help learning about and planting vegetable gardens. And the third is school-themed: two kids need help figuring out what foods make the best kid-friendly, yet nutritious, snacks and lunches they can bring with them to school!
