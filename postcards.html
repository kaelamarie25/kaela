Postcards

direct mail marketing

As part of one of the campaigns launched by the marketing department at NutriSavings, I was tasked with designing postcards to be sent to various potential clients in St. Louis. This project was a lot of fun, plus I had complete creative freedom. I illustrated the icons and established the color palette based on our brand guidelines, and I threw in a few unique design elements (e.g. the slanted lines) to give them a modern, sophisticated feel. Imagery and fonts were selected to highlight the approachablility and friendliness of the NutriSavings brand.
