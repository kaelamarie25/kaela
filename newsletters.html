Monthly HTML Newsletters

digital marketing

Every month, the marketing department sends out a newsletter that corresponds to the National Health Observances set by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services. When I first started, our newsletters looked incredibly outdated, and didn't get many clicks. As I taught myself more and more about HTML emails, I began crafting ways to improve our newsletter, and to turn it into a lead generating tool. Among other changes, I made the newsletter responsive, started using more impactful imagery and CTA copy, and switched up the amount and type of content that we include (such as including more infographics, like this one). I designed and developed from scratch the template that is currently used, and we have already started to see leads generated from it! Goal accomplished.
