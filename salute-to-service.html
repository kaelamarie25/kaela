Salute To Service

promotion

At Spartan Race we wanted to do a small Veteran's Day-inspired promotion that offers veterans and people in the military a 25% discount on a race. I found a stock image of the flag with the two dog tags, and photographed the 'Kids' and 'Honor Series' medals myself. Manipulated them and the vector logos of the 'Hurricane Heats' in the dog tags in Photoshop to create the image.
