AquaDuck

animation

This project was one of the last I completed for a computer animation course I took in college. The prompt was to conceptualize, design, and animate a logo. Going into the project, I knew I wanted to design something fun and whimsical, almost childlike. Immediately, a rubber duck came to mind, and the rest flowed from there. I designed all the assets in Adobe Illustrator, found sound bites from various free sources, and animated it with Adobe Edge Animate.
